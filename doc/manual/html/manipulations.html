Manipulations¶

Manipulations alter trees and tree-associated data. Manipulations can be carried out as part of the queue or from the top-level Data Mr aniulation menu. Unless otherwise stated or set by the manner of execution, manipulations always act upon the currrent or default tree.

Pruning and extinction¶

A common use-case with MeSA is the deletion of terminal tips to simulate the extinction or other loss of taxa.
